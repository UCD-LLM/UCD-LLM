To ensure the convenience of citizens' affairs, it is necessary to establish an online application channel so that citizens can directly submit various applications online. In the submission process, citizens need to strictly complete three operations: fill out the application form in a standardized manner to ensure that all contents are detailed and accurate; Upload all necessary supporting documents as required, and the documents must be clear, legible, and comply with formatting standards; At the same time, it is necessary to go through the real name authentication process to confirm the validity of the applicant's identity. For the application materials submitted online by citizens, the approvers need to conduct a comprehensive review. One is to check the completeness of the materials, confirm whether the application form and various supporting documents are complete and without any missing items; The second is to verify the authenticity of the information, verify the consistency and authenticity between the content filled in the application form and the uploaded proof documents, and eliminate the situation of false information.

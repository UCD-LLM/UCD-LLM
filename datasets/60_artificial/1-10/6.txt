The e-commerce management system should be able to meet the following requirements:
User login and registration function: Users can log in to the system with their username and password, and register new accounts.
Product management function: Administrators can add, modify, and delete product information, and set attributes such as price and inventory of products.
Order management function: Users can browse and select purchased products, and generate orders. Administrators can view and manage the status of orders, such as confirming payment, shipping, cancellation, and other operations.
Marketing activity function: Administrators can create and manage promotional activities, such as discounts and promotions, to attract users to purchase products.
Payment and logistics functions: Users can choose appropriate payment methods and query the logistics information of orders.
